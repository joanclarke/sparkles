Our Services

At Sparkles Events we start with an in-depth, one-on-one consultation where we learn exactly what your dream event looks like. Please don’t hesitate to contact us for an initial consultation or any questions about our services.

Consultation

As creative visionaries behind Sparkles Events and leading authorities in luxury event planning, Sparkles consultants are available to individuals and companies for independent consulting services. Fees vary based on event size and location.

For more information or to schedule an initial consultation, please email sparklesevents@sparkles.com

Design

Sparkles Events’ design talent, under the leadership of Creative Director Shelane Delgado, is renowned. Our dedicated team works closely with clients to define their unique vision, from creating detailed design plans and storyboards that provide a clear visual representation, to the selection and procurement of all design components for review.

Planning

Sparkles Events provide a fully prepared logistics team and unlimited hours of service to supervise the timely installation of all elements.

Our team is there to receive creative partner and vendor deliveries, manage set-up of decor, infrastructure, staging and entertainment, provide guest assistance, cue activities for a successful event flow, liaise with venue management, and conduct final strike.
